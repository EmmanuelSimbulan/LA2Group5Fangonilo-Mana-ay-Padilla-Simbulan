Our Story

The name "Patty's Point" was chosen as a tribute to Patricia, one of the co-founders of the restaurant. Her passion for creating unique and delicious dishes inspired the menu and the concept of showcasing different cuisines. With a focus on quality ingredients, handcrafted cocktails, and a relaxed atmosphere, Patty's Point quickly became a popular destination for locals and tourists alike. From intimate dinners to lively group gatherings, there's always a reason to come back and enjoy the best of what the city has to offer at Patty's Point. Patty's Point is more than just a resto bar - it's a culinary experience that takes your taste buds on a journey around the world. With a menu that showcases an eclectic mix of cuisines, from Asian fusion to European classics, there's something for everyone at Patty's Point. But Patty's Point is more than just great food - it's also a place to create memories with friends and loved ones. It all started in 2022, when four classmates - Shawn, Hannah, Patricia, and Yman - decided to turn their passion for food into a business. Their goal was simple: to create a space where people could come together, relax, and enjoy the finer things in life. And that's exactly what they've done. From their signature cocktails to their mouth-watering barkada meals, everything at Patty's Point is designed to help you unwind and have a good time. So why not come and experience it for yourself? Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or just looking for a night out with friends, Patty's Point is the perfect destination.

Our Chef

  • Chef Hannah Padilla

    With over a decade of experience in the culinary industry, Chef Hannah Padilla is known for her innovative twists on classic dishes. She is constantly experimenting with new ingredients and techniques to create unique flavor profiles that surprise and delight diners.

  • Chef Shawn Michael Mana-ay

    Chef Shawn Michael Mana-ay is a master of fusion cuisine, blending flavors and cooking styles from around the world to create dishes that are truly one-of-a-kind. He is passionate about using fresh, seasonal ingredients and is always on the lookout for new and interesting ingredients to incorporate into his recipes.

  • Chef Patricia Ysabel Fangonilo

    Chef Patricia Ysabel Fangonilo is the heart and soul of Patty's Point. Her passion for cooking is evident in every dish she creates, and her dedication to quality and attention to detail ensure that every meal that leaves her kitchen is nothing short of perfection. She specializes in traditional Filipino cuisine, putting her own unique spin on classic dishes to create something truly special.

  • Chef Emmanuel Simbulan

    Chef Emmanuel Simbulan has traveled the world, honing his skills and learning new techniques from some of the best chefs in the business. He brings that international flair to the kitchen at Patty's Point, creating dishes that are inspired by cuisines from all over the globe. His passion for food is contagious, and his creative energy is evident in every dish he creates.
